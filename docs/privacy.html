Introduction

Welcome to Block ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Account Info Email address, display name (optional), password (encrypted)
Habit Data Habit names, categories, colors, check-ins, streaks, notes

Automatically Collected

Usage Data App interactions, screen views, device info, app version
Technical Data IP address (anonymized), device identifiers, error logs, performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

  1. Provide Services — Create and manage your account, store and sync your habit data, track streaks and calculate statistics, send notifications and reminders.
  2. Improve the App — Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs and crashes, develop new features, optimize performance.
  3. Communications — Send transactional emails (password reset, etc.), provide customer support, send important updates (with your consent).
  4. Business Operations — Process payments for Premium subscriptions, prevent fraud and abuse, comply with legal obligations.

Data Storage and Security

Storage

Your data is stored using Firebase/Firestore (Google Cloud infrastructure), with encryption at rest and in transit. Data is stored in the United States (Google Cloud regions).

Security Measures

  • HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission
  • Secure password hashing
  • Role-based access control
  • Automated security scanning and regular backups

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data stored while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Inactive Accounts: Deleted after 2 years of inactivity (with email notice)

Your Rights

You own your data. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — Update or correct your information
  • Deletion — Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability — Export your habit data (Premium feature, JSON/CSV)
  • Objection — Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw Consent — Revoke permissions at any time

To exercise your rights, email us at support@blockapp.co or use in-app settings under Profile. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Block is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

International Users

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union: our legal basis is consent and contract performance. You have the right to contact your local supervisory authority.

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

For users in California: you have the right to know what data we collect, request data deletion, and opt out of data sale. We do not sell your data. We do not discriminate for exercising your rights.

Cookies and Tracking

  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics (anonymized)
  • Functional: Session management and authentication
  • No Advertising: We do not use advertising trackers

You can disable analytics in app settings or clear app data to remove local storage.

Your Habit Data

Your habit data is private (only visible to you), encrypted, owned by you, and permanently deleted when you delete your account.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Social security numbers
  • Financial information (handled by app stores)
  • Biometric data
  • Location data (unless you enable reminders)
  • Contact lists or photos

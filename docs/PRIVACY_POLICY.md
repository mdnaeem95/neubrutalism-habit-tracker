# Privacy Policy for Block

**Last Updated**: February 5, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Block ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**:
- Email address
- Display name (optional)
- Password (encrypted)

**Habit Data**:
- Habit names and descriptions
- Habit categories and colors
- Check-in dates and times
- Streak information
- Notes attached to habits (Premium feature)

### Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data**:
- App interactions and features used
- Screen views and navigation patterns
- Device information (type, OS version)
- App version and build number

**Technical Data**:
- IP address (anonymized)
- Device identifiers (for analytics)
- Error logs and crash reports
- Performance metrics

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Services**:
   - Create and manage your account
   - Store and sync your habit data
   - Track streaks and calculate statistics
   - Send notifications and reminders

2. **Improve the App**:
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and crashes
   - Develop new features
   - Optimize performance

3. **Communications**:
   - Send transactional emails (password reset, etc.)
   - Provide customer support
   - Send important updates (with your consent)

4. **Business Operations**:
   - Process payments for Premium subscriptions
   - Prevent fraud and abuse
   - Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage

Your data is stored using:
- **Firebase/Firestore**: Cloud database (Google Cloud infrastructure)
- **Secure Servers**: Data encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Geographic Location**: United States (Google Cloud regions)

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security:
- **Encryption**: HTTPS/TLS for data transmission
- **Authentication**: Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions
- **Monitoring**: Automated security scanning
- **Backups**: Regular automated backups

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data stored indefinitely while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Inactive Accounts**: Deleted after 2 years of inactivity (with email notice)
